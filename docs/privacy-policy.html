🔥 FuelFind – Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 15, 2025

FuelFind ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information when you use the FuelFind mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

a) Location Data

FuelFind requests access to your device's location (foreground only) to find gas stations near you. Location data is used solely to retrieve nearby stations and is never stored on our servers. We do not track your location in the background.

b) Price Reports You Submit

When you voluntarily submit a gas price report, we collect:

  • The gas station identifier
  • Fuel type and price you reported
  • Payment type (cash or credit)
  • Your approximate location at the time of the report
  • A randomly generated anonymous device ID (see below)
  • A display name you choose (optional, for the leaderboard)

This data is stored in our database (Supabase) and is used to display crowd-sourced gas prices to other users.

c) Anonymous Device ID

We generate a random identifier for your device the first time you use the App. This ID is stored locally on your device and is used to:

  • Prevent duplicate price reports (rate limiting)
  • Track your points and badges on the leaderboard

This ID is not linked to your name, email, Apple ID, Google account, or any other personally identifiable information.

d) Display Name

If you choose to appear on the leaderboard, you can enter a display name. This name is stored alongside your price reports. You can choose any name — it does not need to be your real name.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not require account registration
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or phone number
  • We do not track your location in the background
  • We do not sell your data to third parties

3. Third-Party Services

FuelFind uses the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy:

  • Google Maps / Places API – used to retrieve nearby gas stations. Google Privacy Policy
  • U.S. Energy Information Administration (EIA) – used to retrieve national average fuel prices. No personal data is shared.
  • Supabase – used to store crowd-sourced price reports. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Nominatim / OpenStreetMap – used for reverse geocoding your location into a city name. OSM Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob – used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data per its own policy. Google Privacy Policy

4. Data Retention

Price reports are stored in our database and used to calculate community averages. Reports older than 7 days are excluded from price calculations. We may retain raw report data for up to 90 days for moderation and abuse prevention purposes.

5. Children's Privacy

FuelFind is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

6. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personally identifiable information and reports are linked only to an anonymous device ID, there is no account to delete. If you uninstall the app, your local device ID is removed. If you would like your submitted price reports removed from our database, contact us at the email below.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the effective date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
